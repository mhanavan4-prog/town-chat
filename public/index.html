🏮 Welcome to Lanternside Bay
A living town on the edge of the Thornreach Wilds. Here's the lay of the land:
1
Explore & chat. The open square has no chat — walk through any building's door to step inside and talk to whoever's there. Six buildings, each its own room.
2
Begin your story. Every class has a six-chapter campaign. Open the 📜 Journal to start Chapter 1, and press F at the townsfolk for side quests.
3
Hunt & grow. Creatures roam after dark — fight them for XP and loot. Harvest herbs out in the Wilds through the north portal.
4
Shape your character. Each level-up earns a skill point — spend it in 🌟 Skills, and check 🎒 Inventory → 📊 Stats to see how your gear and skills stack up.
You can reopen the full controls anytime with H (or the ☰ menu).
❓ How to play
🕹️Move — touch anywhere on the LEFT half and drag. Your character runs where you point.
👁️Look — drag on the right side to swing the camera. Dragging while running re-aims your run.
👆Tap a creature to strike it, a plant to harvest, a player to see who they are.
⚔️Big button — attack the nearest enemy.
🦘Jump, purely for style.
✨Ability slots — your three chosen abilities, cooldowns and all. The ✨/📖 button opens the full kit.
🎛️Make it yours — swap which abilities sit on the wheel (button below).
😀Emotes — a ring of quick reactions everyone nearby sees.
📢Voice clip — appears while something is attacking you; play a saved clip to slip away (everyone in earshot hears it!).
🚪Glowing pill — enter buildings, portals, seats, and conversations. It appears when something is close enough.
☰Menu — inventory, journal, Hard Drive, Town Pass, snapshots, and the rest.
💬Chat — indoors only; the bubble shows what you missed.
⌨️W/S walk · A/D turn · Q/E strafe · Space jump
🖱️Drag to look around · click a creature to strike, a plant to harvest, a player for options
⚔️R — attack the nearest enemy
✋F — interact: sit, portals, NPCs, kiosks
✨1–9, 0, -, = — your ability hotbar, in the order YOU choose (customize below)
📜J — journal & campaign · T — emote wheel · P — snapshot a player
📢V — play your armed voice clip to evade while under attack
